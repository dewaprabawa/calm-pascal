Why Tumang Bali

Not a demo. A day inside Balinese food.

Most "cooking classes" sit you down and cook for you. We don't. You'll grind the base genep spice paste, wrap sate lilit on lemongrass, and grill over coconut-husk charcoal — guided step by step by a local family who learned these recipes from their grandmothers.

Born and raised in Ubud, our chefs have shared this table with guests from all over the world. Beginners are welcome — it's about the culture and the laughter as much as the food.
