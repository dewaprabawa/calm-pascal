Simple pricing. Everything included.

One price covers your market tour, the hands-on class, a full meal of everything you cook, a recipe book to take home, and free pickup in the Ubud area.

Morning Class Starts 8:00 AM · includes market tour + rice-field walk IDR 450k / ~$29
Afternoon Class Starts 2:00 PM · hands-on cooking (no market tour) IDR 400k / ~$26
Private / Couples Class Just your group · flexible timing from IDR 650k / ~$42 pp
Children (under 12) Cooking alongside the family 50% off

Prices are per person and indicative — USD figures vary with the exchange rate. Vegetarian and vegan menus are available on every class at no extra cost.
